## 1. Key Events

1.1 The CPALL Board of Directors has unanimously voted against a proposal by the CP Group to transfer three key subsidiaries—Counter Service, Thai Smart Card, and CP Axtra (Ticker: CPAXT, English Name: CP Axtra Public Company Limited, Chinese Name: 正大国际)—into the CP Group's Virtual Bank structure. The board expressed concerns regarding reduced operational flexibility, complex related-party transaction regulations, and potential conflicts of interest that could affect partnerships with other commercial banks. This critical corporate governance issue is scheduled for a decisive vote by shareholders at an extraordinary meeting on May 29, 2026.

1.3 The International Monetary Fund (IMF) has adjusted its forecast for Thailand's GDP growth in 2026 downwards to 1.5% from a previous projection of 1.6%.
