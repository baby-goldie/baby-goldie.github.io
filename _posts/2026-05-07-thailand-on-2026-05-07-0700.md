## 1. Key Events

1.1 The Thai Board of Investment (BOI) has approved six major investment projects totaling 958 billion baht. The headline project is TikTok's massive 842 billion baht data infrastructure expansion across Bangkok, Samut Prakan, and Chachoengsao, aimed at positioning Thailand as a regional digital hub. This investment, led by a local unit of the Chinese tech giant ByteDance, significantly bolsters the country's AI and cloud service capabilities, reflecting growing global investor confidence in Thailand's digital infrastructure sector despite broader economic volatility.

## 3. What to look out for

3.1 The Thai government's upcoming deployment of the 400 billion baht emergency loan stimulus from June to September will be a critical monitorable for domestic consumption and inflation control. Markets will look for signs of effective aid distribution to SMEs and low-income households, while balancing against concerns over rising public debt and long-term fiscal discipline. The success of the 'Thai Chuay Thai Plus' package and its ability to avert stagflation will set the tone for the country's second-half economic performance.
