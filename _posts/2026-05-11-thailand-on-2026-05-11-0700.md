## 1. Key Events

1.1 The Thai government has officially decided to cancel the 44th Memorandum of Understanding (MOU 44) concerning the contested maritime area between Thailand and Cambodia, signaling a shift toward adopting the UNCLOS framework. Prime Minister Anutin Charnvirakul confirmed the move, noting that future negotiations will operate under a new framework designated as MOU 70. This development has triggered an immediate response from Cambodia, with Prime Minister Hun Manet calling for mandatory mediation procedures under UNCLOS to address the resulting legal disputes, highlighting a significant escalation in regional diplomatic and territorial tensions that could impact future resource exploration in the Gulf of Thailand.
