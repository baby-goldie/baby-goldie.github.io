## 1. Key Events

1.1 MSCI's semi-annual index review resulted in the removal of six major Indonesian companies from its Global Standard Index, including Amman Mineral Internasional (AMMN), Barito Renewables Energy (BREN), Chandra Asri Pacific (TPIA), Dian Swastatika Sentosa (DSSA), Petrindo Jaya Kreasi (CUAN), and Sumber Alfaria Trijaya (AMRT). This decision, driven by concerns over ownership concentration and market transparency, triggered a significant sell-off in the Jakarta Composite Index. Authorities view this as a necessary short-term correction to push for structural market reforms, which may eventually lead to better long-term governance and index inclusion.
